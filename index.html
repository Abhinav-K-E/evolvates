About Us
Evolvates community was formed in 2023 by a group of students from St. Joseph's College Devagiri. It mainly focuses on the students and the main goal is on developing new skills and building a platform for accruing knowledge about the various techs for students.
We aim to boost creativity, innovation, leadership qualities and many more by bringing events and competitions for the beginners. We as a community swear to bring out the best of the students for the upcoming tech world.
Our mission is to build a platform for the students who aim to develop their skills and find best job opportunities for the future.The prime aim of the community is to upgrade students to their fullest potential so that they can face the current needs of the job market.
