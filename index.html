About Us
EVOLVATE Community formed in 2023, is formed by a group of students in St . Joseph College Devagiri, which focuses on developing new skills and building a platform for accuring knowledge about various techs for students. It aims to boost creativity , innovation, leadership quality and much more by bringing events and competitions for the beginners to join. The community swore to bring out the best techies in the students for the upcoming technology world.
Our mission is to build a platform for students aiming to develope their skills and find best job oppurtunities for the future.The prime aim of the community is to upgrade the students to their fullest to face the current needs of the job market.
